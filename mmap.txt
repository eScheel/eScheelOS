Scheel'Nix Memory MAP:

LOWER:
    0x1000  - 0x2FFF  : stage2.bin / bios_memory_map / stage2 stack.    (8KB)
    0x3000  - 0x3FFF  : Unused. Reserved?                               (4KB)
    0x4000  - 0xFFFF  : kernel_addr_tmp.                                (32KB)
    0x10000 - 0xFFFFF : Assume reserved for video memory and stuff. 

UPPER:
    0x100000 - 0x200000 : kernel .text .rodata .data .bss 
    0x200000 - 0x400000 : Heap (Seems to be avail for systems I am targeting..)
